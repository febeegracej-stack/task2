# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
To create meaningful visualizations from the Superstore dataset and derive business insights through storytelling.

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Superstore Dataset

## Dashboard Components
- KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Orders)
- Sales by Segment
- Sales by Region
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Sales by Category
- Key Influencers Analysis

## Key Insights
1. Consumer segment contributes the highest sales.
2. West region records the highest sales.
3. Technology is the best-performing category.
4. Sales show a positive trend over the years.
5. Tables and Chairs significantly influence sales.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on Consumer customers.
- Expand successful strategies from the West region.
- Increase investment in Technology products.
- Improve sales performance in lower-performing regions.
